Place in the City

Oil on canvas series

2024

Nguyen Thanh's "Place in the City" series explores the quiet balance between urban dynamism and inner stillness. Through layered compositions and atmospheric depth, these paintings capture the essence of Ho Chi Minh City's vibrant energy while inviting moments of contemplation and emotional resonance.
