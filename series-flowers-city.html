Flowers in the City

Oil on canvas series

2023

The "Flowers in the City" series captures the delicate beauty and vibrant colors of urban flora. Through expressive brushwork and luminous color palettes, these paintings celebrate the resilience of nature within metropolitan spaces, creating moments of serenity and natural wonder.
